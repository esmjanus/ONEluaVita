Function Documentation

NIL color.loadpalette ( )

Loads a palette of 17 basic colors, ready to use (see example). Colors are:

  • red (255,0,0)
  • green (0,255,0)
  • blue (0,0,255)
  • cyan (0,255,255)
  • magenta (255,0,255)
  • yellow (255,255,0)
  • maroon (127,0,0)
  • grass (0,127,0)
  • navy (0,0,127)
  • turquoise (0,127,127)
  • violet (127,0,127)
  • olive (127,127,0)
  • white (255,255,255)
  • gray (127,127,127)
  • black (0,0,0)
  • orange (255,130,0)
  • chocolate (195,65,0)
  • shine (255,255,255,100)
  • shadow (0,0,0,100)
    Returns
    Nothing.
